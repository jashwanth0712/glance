With the advancement of Agents , and coding workflows , building code has become lot easier than ever ,
previously a single feature required lot of effort , so there was a sprint plan on direction to build 
but now we have the power to flush out features into the product much faster 
and manytimes , developers and team divert themselves from what matters and go deep into building things that might not be the first priority

it can be in the form of
- feature that never asked , but we feel its important 
- fixign the tech debt while leaving priority implementation


main problem being , teams do not pause and see whats the progress and evaluate what to build , but continuously build thereby leaving what their initial goal was

things like 
Building an admin dashboard nobody requested , just because lovable / codex can do it 
Refactoring the auth layer during a launch push
Over-engineering an internal API "for scale" while its being used just 100 times a day rn

most important thing now is not what to work on , but knowing what not to work on
